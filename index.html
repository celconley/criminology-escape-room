Criminology Escape Room

Station 1: Crime Scene Investigation

Examine the crime scene evidence and answer the questions below to collect your first clue!



Station 2: Suspect Profiles

Review the profiles of the main suspects and try to identify who is most likely involved in the crime.

A middle-aged businessman, known for his large charity donations but rumored to be involved in shady practices.

The College Student

A young woman, tech-savvy, who may be hiding a big secret related to security systems and hacking.

The Retired Detective

An elderly man with a lot of experience, known for solving high-profile cases in his younger years.

The Social Media Influencer

A young woman, confident and popular online, but might have a dark side behind her curated persona.

Station 3: The Evidence Puzzle

Piece together the evidence and match the clues to the suspects. The puzzle will lead you to the next step in solving the crime!



Station 4: The Final Decision

After reviewing the evidence and suspects, make your final decision. Who committed the crime?
